Who We Are

Building Futures Through Technical Education

Government Polytechnical Institute for Women (GPIW) is dedicated to empowering women through quality technical education. Established with a vision to bridge the gender gap in technical fields, we provide world-class training in IT, Electronics, Architecture, Fashion Design, Commerce, and Office Management.

Our institute fosters innovation, leadership, and excellence, preparing women to excel in their chosen careers and contribute meaningfully to society.

Principal's Message

At GPIW, we believe in nurturing the potential of every woman. Our commitment to excellence in technical education creates leaders who shape tomorrow's world.

"Empowering women through education is the key to building a progressive nation."

— Principal, GPIW

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a premier institution for women's technical education, recognized globally for innovation, quality, and women empowerment in STEM fields.

Our Mission

Providing comprehensive technical programs with industry-relevant skills, fostering research and innovation, and creating women leaders through holistic development.

Sports & Physical Activities

Team Sports Excellence

Volleyball, badminton, and other team sports build teamwork, communication, and leadership skills essential for personal and professional success.

Fitness & Wellness

Regular fitness programs promote physical health, mental well-being, and discipline, helping students maintain balance between academics and activities.

Student Participation

Every student is encouraged to participate in sports events, inter-college tournaments, and fitness challenges to develop confidence and resilience.

Campus Life & Activities

Language Courses

Short Chinese language courses to enhance global communication skills and prepare students for international opportunities.

Auditorium Events

Seminars, awareness sessions, cultural programs, and guest lectures held regularly in our modern auditorium for student enrichment.

Student Engagement

Clubs, workshops, volunteering, and leadership activities foster creativity, collaboration, and community building among students.

Our Journey

Founded with the noble mission of women's technical empowerment, GPIW has grown from humble beginnings to become a leading polytechnic institute. Over the years, we have expanded our programs, modernized our facilities, and achieved remarkable academic success.

Today, GPIW stands tall as a beacon of excellence, with thousands of successful alumni making significant contributions across various industries.
